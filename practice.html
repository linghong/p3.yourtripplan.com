Let's do a vocabulary practice exercise
The test will give you a word and ask you to pick up the correct explaination from the four explanations listed below. If you make a correct answer. This word will be dropped out of the practice library. If you provide a wrong answer, that word will stay longer on the practice library, meaning that you need do it correctly for several time to make it drop out of the practice library.
